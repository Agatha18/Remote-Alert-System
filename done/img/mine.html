Anti Drowning System with Remote Alert

This system is very helpful for saving the life of a person drowning in water. This is done by informing the lifeguard once a swimmer is in trouble and presses a button given to him/her. It will then transmit using RF. Its range would be around 5-6 meters, if under water around 2-4 meters. For this, the system includes transmitting and receiver circuit. The receiver circuit is with the lifeguard to inform that someone needs to be rescued, while the transmitting circuit is with the person in the water
