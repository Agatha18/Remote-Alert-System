Abstract:

This system is very helpful for saving the life of a person drowning in the water. This is done by informing the heartbeat rate of any person in the water to the lifeguard. Our system will monitor the heart rate. It will then transmit using RF. its range would be around 5-6 meters, if under water around 2-4 meters. For this the system includes transmitting and receiver circuit. The receiver circuit is with the lifeguard to inform about the personâ€™s heart beat rate, while the transmitting circuit is with the person in the water.
